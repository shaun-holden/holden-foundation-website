What is RaiseRight?

Turn Everyday Shopping Into Fundraising

RaiseRight (formerly ShopWithScrip) is a fundraising program that lets you earn money for our booster club simply by purchasing gift cards for stores, restaurants, and services you already use. A percentage of every gift card purchase goes directly to supporting our athletes — at no extra cost to you.

Whether it's groceries, gas, dining out, or online shopping — every purchase counts. It's the easiest way to raise funds without asking for donations.

How to Get Started

  1. Download the RaiseRight app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  2. Use our enrollment code to join our organization and create your personal account with your own email address.
  3. Start shopping! Purchase eGift cards for stores you already shop at. A percentage goes directly to our booster club.
  4. Invite friends & family — press the person icon in the top right corner of the app to share via text or email. The more people who shop, the more we raise!
